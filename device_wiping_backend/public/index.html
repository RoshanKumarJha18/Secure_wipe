Data Leakage Risks

Improper disposal of IT assets (e.g., hard drives, laptops) risks data recovery via forensic tools, leading to privacy violations, financial losses, and non-compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, or NIST 800-88. SecureWipe AI ensures irrecoverable erasure.

Wiping Methods

Choose from Quick (1-pass), Full (3-pass), or DoD 5220.22-M (7-pass) wipes. After wiping, a verifiable PDF certificate is generated with Device, Method, Status, Date, ID, Name, and Serial No.

Circular IT Practices

Secure wiping enables safe reuse/recycling of IT assets, reducing e-waste by up to 80% (EPA stats) and supporting NIST 800-88 compliance for sustainable, cost-effective IT management.
